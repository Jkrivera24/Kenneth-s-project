## Phase B — Section 13: Manual cleaning of candle elements

### Critical warning

> Before dismantling cartridge/candles, the automatic filter must be **completely drained** by **automatic back-flushing of all chambers** (manual activation on control box). **Liquid level must be below the cartridge element** before dismantling.

### 13.1 — Soak

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 1 | Remove **whole filter element assembly** | ☐ |
| 2 | Soak in tank with **candle openings facing down** | ☐ |
| 3 | Use approved solvent; contaminants drain downward out of candles | ☐ |

### 13.2 — Solvent & maximum soak time

| Option | Solvent | **Max time** | Selected |
|--------|---------|--------------|:--------:|
| a | BOLL CLEAN 2000 (cold) | **24 h** | ☐ |
| b | Filterclean (Vecom) | **12 h** | ☐ |
| c | Reiniger B85 (Vecom) | **12 h** | ☐ |
| d | Gas oil | **48 h** | ☐ |

**Soak start / end:** _______________

### 13.3 — Drain solvent

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 4 | Remove assembly; place on perforated stand, **openings down** | ☐ |
| 5 | Allow solvent to drain | ☐ |

### 13.4 — Compressed-air blow

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 6 | With **supplied cleaning gun**, blow **inside → outside** through each candle | ☐ |

### 13.5 — Rinse (separate tank only)

> **Warning:** Rinse only in a **separate tank** with **clean** solvent. Used rinse solvent may be used for the **next soaking** only.

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 7 | Immerse in **fresh** cleaner; rinse **up and down** | ☐ |

### 13.6 — Final dry

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 8 | Drain; dry with compressed air **inside → outside** | ☐ |

**Result:** ~**60% clean** (manual method per OEM).

### 13.7 — ~100% clean (optional)

| Step | Action | Done |
|------|--------|:----:|
| 9 | **Type 5.04 HP Cleaning Unit** + BOLL CLEAN 2000 | ☐ |
