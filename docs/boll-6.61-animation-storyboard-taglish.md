## Scene 02 | Filtration — overview (0:10–0:35)

- **Visual:** Cross-section **Bl.1** style; animate medium **down** into change-over housing
- **Arrows:** Into chambers → through candles **outside → inside** → **outlet**
- **Labels:** `OUTSIDE → INSIDE` | `DIRT ON OUTSIDE MESH` | `SLUDGE VALVE CLOSED` | `AIR RECEIVER READY`
- **VO:** *"Section 5: ang medium ay pumapasok sa change-over housing, dumadaan sa candles outside to inside, ang dumi ay sa labas ng mesh, at ang clean fluid ay sa outlet. Sarado ang sludge valve; naka-ready ang air receiver."*

## Scene 03 | ΔP trigger (0:35–0:45)

- **Visual:** Dirt layer thicker; **ΔP** gauge hits setpoint; electrical contact symbol closes
- **VO:** *"Kapag puno na ang contamination, tataas ang differential pressure at magti-trigger ang backflush."*

## Scene 04 | Motor + change-over plug (0:45–1:00)

- **Visual:** **Geared motor** rotates; plug moves from **reserve** (clean) to connect clean side to outlet
- **Animation:** **ΔP needle drops** fast
- **Labels:** `RESERVE CHAMBER ON-LINE` | `CAM + LIMIT SWITCH`
- **VO:** *"Mag-o-on ang geared motor. Iikot ang change-over plug para ang reserve chamber na malinis ang mag-supply — biglang bababa ang delta P."*

---

## Scene 05 | Sludge valve opens (1:00–1:10)

- **Visual:** Dirty chamber **isolated**; **solenoid** ON; **sludge discharge** opens; pressure in chamber drops
- **Note callout:** *Air in upper plug expands — space for displaced fluid*
- **VO:** *"Mag-o-open ang sludge discharge valve para ma-release ang pressure sa dirty chamber."*

---

## Scene 06 | Flushing valve + counter-current (1:10–1:35)

- **Visual:** **Bl.2** style — **flushing valve** opens; air from **LUFTSPEICHER**; arrows **inside → outside** through one candle row
- **Labels:** `COUNTER-CURRENT` | `BACK-FLUSHING FLUID OUT`
- **VO:** *"Pagkatapos mag-open ang flushing valve, ang air mula sa receiver ay magtutulak ng clean fluid na counter-current, inside to outside, para matanggal ang dumi sa mesh at lumabas sa sludge discharge."*

---

## Scene 07 | End of flush + refill (1:35–1:55)

- **Visual:** Sludge valve **closes**; flushing air **stops**; **refill bore** arrow fills chamber; pressure gauge → operating pressure
- **VO:** *"Matapos ang flushing period, magsasara ang sludge valve, titigil ang flushing air, at mare-refill ang chamber hanggang operating pressure."*

---

## Scene 08 | Summary (1:55–2:15)

- **Visual:** Split screen — left **Filtration** (outside→in), right **Backflush** (inside→out)
- **Table on screen:**

| Filtration | Backflush |
|------------|-----------|

| Sludge closed | Sludge open |
| Motor off | Motor on |

- **VO:** *"Filtration: outside to inside, sludge sarado. Backflush: counter-current, sludge bukas, reserve chamber ang nag-su-supply sa engine."*
