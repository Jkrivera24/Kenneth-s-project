How it works

  1. Sounding table — lookup observed volume using sounding, trim, and list correction.
  2. VCF (ASTM 54B) — correct volume to 15°C from observed temperature and density @ 15°C.
  3. Mass — MT = standard volume × density @ 15°C ÷ 1000.
